# Scott Sun

[Visit at scottsun.io](https://scottsun.io)

This repository contains the source for `scottsun.io`, my personal website: part portfolio, part experiment lab, part manifesto.

It is where I collect the things I care about most: frontier AI, building useful and unusual apps, learning in public, and making technology feel ambitious, cinematic, and alive.

## What You'll Find

- **About Me**: a short personal philosophy centered on technology, intelligence, and optimism.
- **Learning Journey**: the path from first picking up programming to building AI, 3D, and creative software projects.
- **Apps**: live products and experiences people can actually explore.
- **Inspirations**: thinkers and builders who shaped how I see work, ambition, and the future.
- **Art in Life**: a virtual gallery of arts captured in life.

## Why This Site Exists

I like building things that feel curious, personal, and a little unexpected. This site is the home for that work. Instead of being a static resume, it is meant to show taste, momentum, and range through actual projects, ideas, and shipped experiences.

If you want the full experience, start here: [scottsun.io](https://scottsun.io)
